Popups and Dialog Boxes

"This page demonstrates GWT's built-in support for in-page popups. The first is a very simple informational popup that closes itself automatically when you click off of it. The second is a more complex draggable dialog box. If you're wondering why there's a list box at the bottom, it's to demonstrate that you can drag the dialog box over it (this obscure corner case often renders iconrrectly on some browsers)."
