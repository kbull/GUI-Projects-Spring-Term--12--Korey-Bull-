Introduction to the Kitchen Sink

"This is the Kitchen Sink sample. It demonstrates many of the widgets in the Qt framework."

"This sample also demonstrates something else really useful in GWT: history support. When you click on a tab, the location bar will be updated with the current history token, which keeps the app in a bookmarkable state. The back and forward buttons work properly as well. Finally, notice that you can right-click a tab and 'open in new window'."
